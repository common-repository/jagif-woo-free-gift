Offer free gifts with purchases using custom rules. Highlight eligible products with visual gift icons to inform and entice customers

== Description ==

If you are looking for something that helps you offer WooCommerce free gift to customers who come to your store and purchase your items, which not only shows your thankfulness to customers for choosing your products but also encourages them to buy more to get the little cute free gifts. This is exactly what you need. Jagif – WooCommerce Free Gift allows the admin to create gift products and give them according to the rules you create when customers buy products.

###Important Notice:

- **The product you choose to be WooCommerce free gift product must be in stock**

- **The product which customers purchase must be in stock, variable or simple product to get gift product**

- **The gift product must be selected all before the customer click the “Add to cart” button**

###FEATURES

With **Jagif – WooCommerce Free Gift**, you can:

&#9658; **Create WooCommerce free gift products**: The plugin displays free gift boxes in single product pages and icons in the WooCommerce store page. The free gift box will notify customers when buying this product will automatically receive what gifts.

- **Set max and min gift pack quantity per one order/product**

- **Choose products for Gift Pack**: Select any in stock products available to be gift pack (can not select out stock products to be gift pack).

&#9658; **Add rules to apply gifts**: After creating Gift Pack, now you want to offer customers these gift packs when they purchase your items.

- **Create rule for product or category** Choose products to be gifted using "INCLUDE". If you want to exclude a few products, choose "EXCLUDE". (You can add multiple Product or Category)

- **Add Gift as you want to each particular rule**

&#9658; **Settings**:In the settings of the plugin, you control the number of items for one pack/product.

- **Enable**:turn this option on to enable features of the plugin.

- **Maximum number of items per pack**:set the maximum number for an item to be included in a gift pack.

- **Maximum gift pack per product**:set the maximum number of gift pack a customer receive for each product in the cart.

&#9658; **Customize gift symbol on the front end in Theme settings**: When you want to offer your customers gifts, you need to have a gift pack first.

- **Customize icon**: you can customize how the icon shows up on the Shop page and on the Single product page.

- **Customize Gift Box in single page**: Customize how the gift card show: with popup window or appears below Add to cart button.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the download package
1. Upload `jagif-woo-free-gift` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
